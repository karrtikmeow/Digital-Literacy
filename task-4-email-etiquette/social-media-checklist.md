# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

### ✅ Do's (Best Practices)
1. **Enable Privacy Settings:** Regularly review and restrict who can see your personal posts and contact information.
2. **Think Before You Post:** Consider if your content is professional and how it might be perceived by future employers or faculty.
3. **Fact-Check Information:** Verify the authenticity of news or academic resources before sharing them with your batchmates.
4. **Network Professionally:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with industry experts and alumni in your field.
5. **Respect Others' Privacy:** Always ask for permission before posting photos or tagging friends in your digital content.

### ❌ Don'ts (Avoid These)
1. **Don't Share Sensitive Data:** Never post your home address, phone number, or official ID details on public profiles.
2. **Don't Engage in Cyberbullying:** Avoid using offensive language or participating in online arguments and harassment.
3. **Don't Click Suspicious Links:** Avoid clicking on "clickbait" or unverified links that could lead to phishing sites.
4. **Don't Plagiarize Content:** Never claim someone else's digital work, art, or writing as your own without proper credit.
5. **Don't Over-Share Location:** Avoid posting real-time "check-ins" that reveal your exact location to strangers.
