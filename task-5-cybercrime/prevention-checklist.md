# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist


### 🛡️ General Cyber Safety Tips
1. **Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Always add an extra layer of security to your university and social media accounts.
2. **Verify the Sender:** Before clicking any links in an "urgent" email, check the sender's full email address for subtle misspellings.
3. **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Avoid using the same password across different platforms; use a mix of symbols, numbers, and letters.
4. **Think Before You Click:** Hover your mouse over any link to see the actual destination URL before clicking it.
5. **Keep Software Updated:** Regularly update your browser and operating system to protect against known security vulnerabilities.
6. **Be Wary of Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid logging into sensitive accounts or making financial transactions while connected to unsecured public networks.

### 💸 UPI & Financial Safety (Mandatory)
7. **No PIN for Receiving Money:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money; a PIN is only required to *send* or *pay* money.
8. **QR Code Caution:** Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown individuals, as these are frequently used in "request money" scams.

---

### 🚨 How to Report Cybercrime in India
If you fall victim to a digital scam or notice suspicious activity, report it immediately through official channels:

* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Helpline:** Call **1930** (Available 24/7)
